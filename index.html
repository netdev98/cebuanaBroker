FAQ

Common Questions

How much is your broker's commission?+
Standard broker's commission follows PRC guidelines — typically 3–5% for property sales and 1 month's rent for lease transactions. We discuss fees transparently before any engagement and confirm in writing.
Are you PRC licensed?+
Yes — I hold an active PRC Real Estate Broker license (REB-0012345, valid until 2026) and am also a Certified Real Property Appraiser (CRPA). All license details are available on request or verifiable via the PRC website.
Is the initial consultation free?+
Absolutely — the initial consultation is completely free. We meet in person or online to understand your needs and present the best options available before any fee is involved.
Can you help OFWs buy property remotely?+
Yes! We assist many OFW clients with remote property purchases. Virtual tours, online document processing, and SPA (Special Power of Attorney) arrangements are all part of our service for overseas buyers.
What documents do I need to buy a property?+
For residential purchases you'll need: valid government ID, TIN, proof of income (ITR or payslip), and bank statements. For OFWs add: employment contract and OFW ID. We guide you through every document from start to title transfer.
How long does a property appraisal take?+
A standard residential appraisal takes 3–5 business days from the site visit to receipt of the signed and sealed appraisal report. Rush appraisals (1–2 days) are available for an additional fee.
Which banks accept your appraisal reports?+
We are accredited appraisers with BDO, BPI, Metrobank, UnionBank, and several rural banks in Cebu. Our reports are accepted for home loans, business loans, and estate settlement valuations.
Can you help me sell my property faster?+
Yes — we use a multi-channel marketing strategy: professional photography, GHL-powered lead generation, social media advertising, and our curated buyer database. Most properties we list receive qualified offers within 30–90 days.
